This includes my time at MSRA (May 2022 – Nov 2023), MSR AI4S Beijing (Mar 2025 – Sep 2025), and most recently MSR AI4S Cambridge (Jan 2026 – May 2026), where I explored the intersection of LLMs and chemistry.
